Our Story

Founded with a vision to break down the silos in traditional financial services, Kidmans Partners has grown into a comprehensive advisory firm.

We recognized early on that business owners and families were frustrated by having to coordinate between separate accountants, wealth managers, and brokers. Information was lost, strategies conflicted, and opportunities were missed.

By bringing these disciplines under one roof, we created a model where your entire financial ecosystem works in harmony. One master plan, executed by a unified team of specialists.
